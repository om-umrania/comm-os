# BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front)

## Concept
The BLUF is the military standard for executive communication. It requires stating the destination, decision, or core takeaway in the very first sentence.

## Why Om Fails at It
Engineers tend to build up to a conclusion. They explain the research, the data, the architecture, and *then* the result. Executives don't have time for the journey; they only need the destination.

## The Standard
- Must be delivered within the first 10-15 seconds of speaking.
- Must be 1 to 2 sentences max.
- Must answer: "So what?" or "What is the decision?"

## Example
**Bad (Engineer's Trap):** "So we looked at the vector database latency, and because cosine similarity takes O(N) over 100k vectors, we realized caching wasn't enough, so we decided to upgrade to Pinecone."
**Good (BLUF):** "We are upgrading to Pinecone to solve our latency issues."
